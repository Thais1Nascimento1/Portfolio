Thais Nascimento

When I was two years old, my parents decided to move our family from Brazil to the United States in search of a better life. I grew up seeing them work hard to provide for my brother and me, which taught me great work ethic and dedication to others. Back then, my parents and fellow immigrants would turn to me to translate documents between English and Portuguese or to help other children with their homework. Today, as a mother of three, I want to continue modeling my family’s legacy of commitment to others and paving a way for all immigrants. And as a software engineer, I’m excited to build solutions that will improve and uplift people’s lives.
